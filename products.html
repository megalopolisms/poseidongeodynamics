SKU Decoder

RS 2875 - 10 12 - 5 G
RS = Type (Round Shaft)
2875 = Shaft OD (2.875")
10 12 = Helix diameters (10" + 12")
5 = Lead length (5 ft)
G = Galvanized (hot-dip zinc per ASTM A153)
